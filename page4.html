Review from TripAdvisor

Best trout ever. Nice atmosphere, great place and service. 

I recommend this place if you want to eat a fresh fish (trout): that is raised by the restaurant in their yard. It was the best I ever served. Very tasty and fast served.

Ovidiu R                         

The trout experience!

We lats visited the restaurant, with family, for lunch. I was really impressed with their renovations and extension and the food was delicious. They don’t have a large variety of dishes but what they have is very tasty. It is a treat for the people that love trout. I highly recommend the trout borsch, the fried trout with polenta as well as both of their desserts: the pancakes and the traditional donuts(papanasi). But to be honest everything I ate there at one time or another was delicious. And a plus also to the chefs for the speed of preparation as well as to our waiter for the quick delivery and nice atmosphere.

Excelent dishes

We went in the summer and really apreciated the site. The menu is exactly what you would expect: they only have dishes with trouts. We tried the fish sour soup and the grilled trout and they were absolutely delicious. We also recommend the mushrooms sote and the pancakes. The cuisine is worth the trip and the service was all right.

Mandatory pit-stop on the way home

Once again we were skiing in Northern Moldavia. And, once again, on our way back home we have done our mandatory pit-stop for the trout. The king of cold mountain water. The options are narrow down to one pan-fried, two grilled specialties and a smoked ones. But this is enough because it all rest in the taste. And now in a new and improoved location. A wider room is now availible maybe for big events too. If in high season, be sure to make prior reservations. Enjoy!
