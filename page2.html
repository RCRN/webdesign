We are a small business that want to provide to the customers the real taste of a fresh trout. We started this business 7 years ago, first with only 8 table and now, after all this time we are really prepare to offer to every one of you a table.

We offer you a really unique experience, our restaurant is entaerly made of wood with some craved bears and a hunting appearance, like the one you see in a castle dining room. 

Our menu is almost based on trout, the trout that you can see on the fish pond right next the parking lot. But because we are into a mountain area, and we have the blood of a hunter, we also have some wild game stew. And in our way back home we also take the mushrooms for a delicious and complet dinner . 

At the foot of the mountain, after the river, in a place named Pojorata lays our House of the Trout.
